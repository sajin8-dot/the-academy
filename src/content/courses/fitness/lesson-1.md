# Fitness Fundamentals

Welcome to the Fitness course. This is where you build the foundation for a healthier, stronger you.

## Consistency is Key

The most important aspect of any fitness journey is consistency. You don't need to be perfect every day, but you do need to show up.

## The Pillars of Fitness

- **Nutrition:** Fueling your body correctly.
- **Training:** Stimulating muscle growth and cardiovascular health.
- **Recovery:** Allowing your body to heal and adapt.
