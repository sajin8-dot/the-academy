# Intro to Piano and Sheet Music

Welcome to the Piano and Sheet Music course. This curriculum consists of 29 sessions across 4 modules.

## Module 1: The Basics

In this first session, we will familiarize ourselves with the keyboard layout and the basic concepts of reading sheet music.

### The Keyboard Layout

The piano keyboard consists of white and black keys. The pattern of black keys repeats in groups of twos and threes.
