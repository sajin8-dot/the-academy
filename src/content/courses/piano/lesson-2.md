# Lesson 2: Your First Song

They want you to memorize lines and spaces. They want you to decode a map before you've even walked the street. Forget that.

We’re playing a song. Not because we’re "ready," but because the air is waiting for it. We’re using a melody written by a man who couldn't even hear it when he finished it—Beethoven. His *Ode to Joy*.

It’s built on simple steps. No leaps. No gymnastics. Just a steady climb and a gentle fall.

[START DRONE: frequency=261.63, volume=0.3, waveform="warm-pad"]

---

## The Climb (The First Phrase)

Listen to the first four notes. It’s a walk up the stairs, and then a look back down.

[PLAY MIDI: sequence=["E4", "E4", "F4", "G4"], instrument="piano"]

"Now, you find those steps. Start on the note that feels like a reach from Home."

[AWAIT MIDI: sequence=["E4", "E4", "F4", "G4"], timeout=15000]
[ON SUCCESS: BLOOM intensity=medium, color="gold"]
[ON FAILURE: feedback="You're stumbling on the stairs. Listen to the climb again. E, E, F, G."]

---

## The Fall (The Resolution)

Every climb needs a landing. Listen to how it settles back toward the center.

[PLAY MIDI: sequence=["G4", "F4", "E4", "D4"], instrument="piano"]

"Bring it down. Don't rush the descent."

[AWAIT MIDI: sequence=["G4", "F4", "E4", "D4"], timeout=15000]
[ON SUCCESS: BLOOM intensity=medium, color="cyan"]

---

## The Anchor (The Final Rest)

Finally, we hit the ground.

[PLAY MIDI: sequence=["C4", "C4", "D4", "E4", "E4", "D4", "D4"], instrument="piano"]

"Walk it all the way Home."

[AWAIT MIDI: sequence=["C4", "C4", "D4", "E4", "E4", "D4", "D4"], timeout=20000]
[ON SUCCESS: BLOOM intensity=high, color="green"]

---

## The Full Bridge: Ear-to-Hand-to-Eye

Now, and *only* now, look at what you just played. This is how the world writes down the feeling you just had.

## Cobain's Reflection

Beethoven was deaf when he wrote this. He wasn't looking at a piano; he was listening to the vibration of the universe in his skull. When you play this, don't just hit the keys. Feel the wood. Feel the air move.

You just played your first song.
