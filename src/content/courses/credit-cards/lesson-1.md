# The Basics of Credit Cards

Welcome to the first lesson of the Credit Cards course. In this lesson, we will cover the fundamental concepts of credit cards.

## What is a Credit Card?

A credit card is a payment card issued to users to enable them to pay a merchant for goods and services based on the cardholder's promise to the card issuer to pay them for the amounts plus the other agreed charges.

## Why use a Credit Card?

- Building credit history
- Earning rewards
- Fraud protection
- Convenience
